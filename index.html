Each company needs its mission but also the principles that should guide and to be followed. Our goals are very very clearly defined for whole team. We just know what we want to achieve!
01.
Getting the most out of existing investments: Discover the potential of your OnPrem infrastructure. Reduce under utilised machines. Reveal over provisioned storage.
02.
Understand your estate and maximise it's potential: Bring together the data from all areas of your estate in one place. Gather data from the Tin up and from the team owners down.
03.
Place machines OnPrem and in the cloud wherever is most cost effective:Use the hypervisor agnostic placement engine to find the best location for your machines.
04.
API driven Infrastructure As A Service: Developers generate API keys and provision on the fly. Define standard machine sizes. Use standard images for immutable builds across all teams and Hypervisors.
05.
Platform As A Service offering: Utilise an intuitive UI for development teams to manage their estate. Tap into collective Single point of data for the business.
06.
Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment: Integrate the API and PaaS with your existing deployment pipeline to choose the best machine build location when you deploy your code.

Hypervisors and Technology

Directory Services Automation
Automate the provisioning of users, groups, permissions and policies.




Resource Scanner
Scan your entire virtualisation investment with a hypervisor agnostic service that perpetually collects metrics such as RAM, CPU and storage utilisation. Seamlessly introduce new capacity, automatically open and close resource pools and more.
Tin Scanner
Gain visibility from the ground up, allowing you to understand the hardware on which your estate runs. In one location understand the OS, version, patch ref, availablitiy, used storage, last use and power status of all your machines.

Intelligent Resource Placement
Allocate compute resource based on strategies that matter to you. Favour redundancy? deploy a particular service across many nodes. Favour cost savings? deploy to the cheapest hardware. Create your own custom policies that can be applied globally or to specific environments or platforms. Combine with the Resource Scanner for placement.

UNDERSTAND THE COSTS
Apply costs to your OnPrem infrastructure and track those of your Cloud investments. Gain reporting at a high and low level to understand cost per team, service, hypervisor and more. Discover unused and under utilised resources and put them to better use. Combine with the Resource Scanner / Tin Scanner to see where Teams are over provisioning resource and tighten up your Deploy/Destroy cycle.
